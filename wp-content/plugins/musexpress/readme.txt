== Description ==

MusexPress is the first software to merge Adobe Muse and the Wordpress admin panel into one powerful tool. Get a very intuitive and simple admin panel without renouncing the absolute design freedom. You can design your layout in Adobe Muse and manage any content with WordPress. From now on your customers can seamlessly create, edit, review and publish content themselves.

= Manage anything from a single dashboard =

You can add, move or delete any texts, images, audio and video on your site without even opening the Muse project. You can also manage the Blog and e-Commerce from the same dashboard without jumping back to Muse!

= Tons of Muse Widgets for a highly styleable site =

You can use all the Muse widgets you love to give your website the desired look and feel and customize every aspect of the user experience. Enjoy a limitless design freedom to give your website a unique style together with a high level of usability

= Compatible with every Muse Template =

Whether you want to design your own template from scratch, or you need to save time and use a pre-styled theme, you can add MusexPress to the Muse template in almost non time to get a powerful CMS to easily manage your website.

= A good help for a perfect SEO =

Don’t miss the way to boost your SEO rankings and watch your website rise the ranks to the top of search-engine results. With MusexPress 3.0. you have friendly URLs for all the website pages (WooCommerce product pages and blog posts included).

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =
* PHP version 5.6.0 or greater (PHP 7.1 or greater is recommended)
* MySQL version 5.6 or greater

= Recommended Server Modules =
* ZipArchive
* MySQLi
* Mbstring

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don’t need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of MusexPress, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

Click on the upload plugin button and select your musexpress.zip file, after the process is complete you simply need to press on "Activate Now" button.

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our MusexPress plugin and uploading it to your webserver via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).

= Updating =

To enable automatic updates you need to verify your *MuseGain ID*, through the MusexPress Settings that you can find under MusexPress Menu on your WordPress Dashboard.

== FAQ ==

= What is MusexPress ? =
The first and mighty solution to match Adobe Muse with WordPress.
MusexPress is a free WordPress plugin that allows you to design websites in [Adobe Muse](http://muse.adobe.com/) and manage any contents with WordPress.

= How can I get MusexPress CMS? =
MusexPress is totally FREE. You can download it directly from MuseGain.com with our Blog (Lite version). If you want to use our official plugins and templates, you can buy them individually or subscribe our Advanced, Rockstar or Lifetime plan to have more than 250 Muse tools.

= Can i use WordPress third party plugins with MusexPress? =
Sure! You can use all the WordPress Third Party Plugins with Adobe Muse through our Boxes and Page Creator plugins and manage them from the WordPress admin panel.

= Where can I get support or talk to other users? =
If you get stuck, you can ask for help on the [MusexPress Plugin Forum](https://www.musegain.com/forums) or on the [Official MusexPress Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/musexpress.cms/).
For any help with MusexPress premium plugins (payment products) on MuseGain, you can use our [helpdesk](https://www.musegain.com/help/).

= Where I can find a complete documentation and video tutorials ? =
You can find everything you need to start with MusexPress on our [documentation section](https://www.musegain.com/documentation) or you can learn how MusexPress works with our [video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCozlJtbWQao1N_ViGp7BmuA).

= How can I design my website in Adobe Muse and then use it with WordPress ? =
The only things that you need to do when your Adobe Muse website is ready to go online is to install the MusexPress plugin in WordPress, then upload the Muse website on your server and finally convert it with MusexPress.
Thats' all!
